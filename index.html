Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my appointment?

+

We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance for regular services, and 1-2 months ahead for bridal packages or special events. However, we do accommodate same-day appointments when available.

Do you offer consultations?

+

Yes! We offer complimentary 15-minute consultations for all new clients, especially for major hair color changes or bridal makeup. This ensures we understand your vision and can recommend the best services.

What products do you use?

+

We use premium, professional-grade products including Redken, Olaplex, and Moroccan Oil for hair services; OPI and CND for nails; and MAC and Bobbi Brown for makeup. All products are cruelty-free and selected for their quality and performance.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

We require 24-hour notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may be charged 50% of the service price. We understand that emergencies happen, so please contact us as soon as possible if you need to reschedule.

Do you offer group discounts for events?

+

Yes! We offer special packages for bridal parties, prom groups, and other events. Groups of 4 or more receive a 10% discount, and groups of 6+ receive 15% off. Contact us to customize your group experience.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept cash, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), and mobile payments. We also offer gift cards if you'd like to purchase services for someone special.
